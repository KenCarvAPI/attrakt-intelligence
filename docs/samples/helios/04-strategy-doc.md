# Helios — 2026 Strategy (Internal)

## Thesis
Stablecoins crossed into mainstream settlement in 2025. The winners will be the
infrastructure layers, not the consumer apps. Helios bets on being the default
developer rails before incumbents adapt.

## Strategic priorities (ranked)
1. Developer adoption over enterprise logos in H1 — bottoms-up wins this market.
2. DeFi developers are the wedge: highest pain, fastest to integrate, loudest
   advocates. Own this segment before expanding to mainstream fintech.
3. Compliance as a moat — ship travel-rule + KYB tooling competitors lack.

## Positioning bets
- "Stripe for stablecoins" as the category frame.
- Lead with developer experience and uptime, not crypto narratives.

## Upcoming bets
- Q2: Payments API GA + a hosted checkout component.
- Q3: EU settlement expansion; first regulated partner.
- Q4: Payouts product for marketplaces.

## What we will not do
No token. No consumer wallet. No yield products. These would compromise our
neutral-infrastructure positioning and our compliance posture.
