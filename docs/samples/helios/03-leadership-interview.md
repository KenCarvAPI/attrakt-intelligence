# Leadership Interview — Helios (CEO & CPO)

## Product vision (CEO)
We want stablecoin payments to be a boring, solved problem. In three years a
developer should add global money movement in an afternoon and never think about
chains, gas, or compliance again. Helios is the rails; our customers are the
brand their users see.

## 12-month priorities (CEO)
1. Launch the Payments API to general availability.
2. Win the DeFi developer community as our first beachhead — they feel the pain
   most acutely and they tell each other what works.
3. Land three reference fintech customers in the EU.

## Positioning (CPO)
Our competition is not other crypto companies — it is the status quo of building
payments in-house, and legacy processors that don't speak stablecoins. We are
neutral infrastructure. We will never compete with our customers by launching a
consumer wallet.

## What we will never do (CEO)
- Never launch a token. Helios is infrastructure, not a speculation vehicle.
- Never custody customer funds beyond the settlement window.
- Never trade reliability for growth — uptime is the product.

## What success looks like (CEO)
Developers recommending us unprompted in their communities, and a settlement
volume chart that goes up and to the right without a single incident page.
