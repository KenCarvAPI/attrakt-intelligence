HELIOS BRAND GUIDELINES

Personality: the calm, competent infrastructure company. We are the boring,
reliable layer other people build exciting things on. Confident, never loud.

Tone: precise, developer-first, plainspoken. We explain, we don't hype. We
respect the reader's time and intelligence.

Vocabulary we use: rails, settlement, primitives, stablecoins, predictable,
developer experience, uptime, proofs.

Things we NEVER say:
- "Revolutionary", "game-changing", "to the moon", "disrupt".
- Anything implying yield, returns, or that stablecoins are an investment.
- "Crypto" as a vibe — we say "stablecoins" or "digital dollars" concretely.
- Price/token speculation of any kind.

One-liner: predictable money rails for builders.
