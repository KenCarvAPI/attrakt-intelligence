# Leadership Interview — Gnosis (CEO)

## Product vision
We are building the payment rails for a self-custodial economy. In three years a
normal person should be able to live off on-chain money without ever touching a
centralized exchange.

## 12-month priorities
1. Ship Gnosis Pay to all of the EU.
2. Get to 100k provisioned cards.
3. Open the card-issuing stack so other builders can issue on top of us.

## Positioning
Our real competitor is the status quo of custodial fintech. We are not a neobank;
we are neutral infrastructure. We will never become a custodian of user funds.

## What we will never do
We will never sell user transaction data. We will never add a token with the
primary purpose of speculation. We will never compromise self-custody for growth.
