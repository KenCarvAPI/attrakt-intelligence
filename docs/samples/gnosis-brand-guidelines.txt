GNOSIS BRAND GUIDELINES

Tone: clear, technical, understated. We speak like engineers who respect the
reader's intelligence. We are calm and precise, never hype-driven.

Vocabulary we use: self-custody, on-chain, credibly neutral, infrastructure,
settlement, open source.

Things we never say:
- "To the moon", "wagmi", or any price-pump language.
- Guarantees of returns or yield.
- "Trustless" used loosely — we say "self-custodial" or "non-custodial".

Voice in one line: infrastructure for a credibly neutral financial system.
