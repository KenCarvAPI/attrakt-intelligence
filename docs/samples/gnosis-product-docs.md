# Gnosis Pay — Product Overview

Gnosis Pay is a self-custodial payments product that links a Visa debit card to
an on-chain account. Users keep custody of their funds in a smart account and
spend directly from it at any merchant that accepts Visa.

## Who it serves
- Crypto-native users who want to spend stablecoins in the real world without
  off-ramping through a centralized exchange.
- EU residents (initial market) holding EURe and other stablecoins.

## Key differentiators
- Non-custodial: the user's keys, the user's funds, all the way to the point of sale.
- Settlement on Gnosis Chain for low fees.
- No KYC to hold the account; KYC only to provision the Visa card.
