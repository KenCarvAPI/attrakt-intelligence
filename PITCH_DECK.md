## Slide 3: Key Features - Unified View

### See Your Community as One

**Cross-Platform Identity Resolution**
- Automatically links the same person across Discord, GitHub, Twitter
- Works even with different usernames
- Email, username, and wallet address matching
- Confidence scoring for matches

**Complete Member Profiles**
- Total engagement across all platforms
- Activity patterns and trends
- Contribution history
- First seen, last seen, engagement timeline

**Example:**
- Discord: `JohnDoe#1234` → 500 messages
- GitHub: `johndoe` → 50 commits, 10 PRs
- Twitter: `@john_doe` → 200 tweets
- **Attrakt shows:** John Doe = 1 person, 750 total interactions

## Slide 5: Key Features - Threat Detection

### Proactive Protection with AI

**Automated Threat Scanning**
- Scans all messages and content 24/7
- AI-powered analysis using Claude (Anthropic)
- Real-time alerts for threats
- Evidence collection for moderation

**Threat Categories:**
1. **Harassment** - Personal attacks, slurs, threats
2. **Impersonation** - Fake accounts, similar usernames
3. **Spam** - Repeated messages, suspicious links
4. **Coordinated Attacks** - Unusual volume from new accounts
5. **FUD** - Spreading fear, uncertainty, doubt

**Alert System:**
- Instant Slack notifications for critical threats
- Email alerts with detailed reports
- Threat severity scoring (LOW/MEDIUM/HIGH/CRITICAL)
- Evidence and context for each detection

**Result:** Catch problems before they escalate.
