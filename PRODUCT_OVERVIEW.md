# Attrakt: Community Intelligence Platform

**Turn community data into actionable insights**

---

## The Problem

Managing a thriving community across Discord, GitHub, and Twitter is incredibly difficult. You're drowning in data but starved for insights:

- **Who are your most engaged members?** You don't know because they use different platforms with different usernames
- **What's the health of your community?** You're guessing based on gut feelings
- **Are there threats or bad actors?** You find out too late, after damage is done
- **What trends should you pay attention to?** You're too busy moderating to analyze patterns

**You need a unified view of your community across all platforms.**

---

## The Solution: Attrakt

Attrakt is a **Community Intelligence Platform** that gives you a complete, unified view of your community across Discord, GitHub, and Twitter. We automatically track, analyze, and protect your community so you can focus on building, not managing.

### How It Works

1. **Connect Your Platforms** - Link your Discord server, GitHub repositories, and Twitter accounts
2. **Automatic Tracking** - We continuously monitor messages, events, and engagement across all platforms
3. **Identity Resolution** - We intelligently link the same person across platforms, even with different usernames
4. **AI-Powered Analysis** - Get daily insights, threat detection, and community health metrics
5. **Actionable Insights** - Receive daily digests with what matters most

---

## Key Features

### 🔍 Unified Community View

**See one person across all platforms**
- Automatically identifies the same member across Discord, GitHub, and Twitter
- Links identities even when usernames differ (e.g., `johndoe` on GitHub vs `JohnDoe#1234` on Discord)
- Tracks complete engagement history regardless of platform

**Comprehensive Member Profiles**
- Total engagement across all platforms
- Contribution patterns and activity trends
- Cross-platform behavior analysis
- First seen, last seen, and activity timelines

### 📊 Real-Time Analytics

**Community Health Metrics**
- Daily Active Users (DAU) across all platforms
- Message volume and engagement trends
- Sentiment analysis (positive, neutral, negative)
- Growth rates and member acquisition trends
- Top contributors and most active members

**Platform-Specific Insights**
- Discord: Channel activity, reactions, joins/leaves
- GitHub: Commits, PRs, issues, stars, forks
- Twitter: Mentions, engagement, follower growth

**Time-Series Data**
- Historical trends and patterns
- Week-over-week and month-over-month comparisons
- Anomaly detection (unusual spikes or drops)

### 🛡️ AI-Powered Threat Detection

**Proactive Protection**
- Automatically scans all messages and content for threats
- Detects harassment, spam, impersonation, and coordinated attacks
- Real-time alerts for high-severity threats
- Evidence collection for moderation actions

**Threat Categories**
- **Harassment**: Personal attacks, slurs, threats of violence
- **Impersonation**: Fake accounts, similar usernames
- **Spam**: Repeated messages, suspicious links
- **Coordinated Attacks**: Unusual volume from new accounts
- **FUD**: Spreading fear, uncertainty, and doubt

**Smart Alerts**
- Instant notifications for critical threats (Slack, Email)
- Detailed threat reports with evidence
- Context and analysis for each detection

### 📈 Daily Community Pulse

**AI-Generated Daily Digests**
- Automatically generated every morning
- Key metrics and trends from the past 24 hours
- Anomalies and noteworthy events
- Top contributors and highlights
- Sentiment analysis and community health score

**Delivered Where You Need It**
- Slack notifications
- Email digests
- Available in dashboard (coming soon)

### 🔗 Cross-Platform Identity Resolution

**Intelligent Matching**
- Email-based matching (most reliable)
- Exact username matching
- Fuzzy username matching (handles variations)
- Wallet address matching (for crypto/Web3 communities)
- Confidence scoring for each match

**Why This Matters**
- Understand true engagement levels
- Identify your super-users and advocates
- Better moderation decisions
- More accurate analytics
